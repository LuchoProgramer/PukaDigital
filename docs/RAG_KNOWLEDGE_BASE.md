## 4. Methodology (The "Why")
**Ingeniería Inversa de Ventas (Reverse Sales Engineering):**
Most agencies design first and try to sell later. We do the opposite:
1.  **Minería de Datos (Keywords):** We research what people are *already* searching for in Google (Volume & CPC).
2.  **Arquitectura Web:** We build the site specifically to answer those search queries (Relevance).
3.  **Tráfico de Precisión:** We launch Google Ads with 10/10 Quality Score because the site matches the ads perfectly.
*   *Result:* Lower CPC, Higher Conversion, More Sales.
